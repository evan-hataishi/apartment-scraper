Luxury San Jose Apartments


Our floor plans easily adapt to your needs, allowing you to choose the one that best suits you and decorate it after your own heart. Depending on what you are aiming for, you can go for an efficient studio that ranges between 550 and 562 square feet. Or opt for a more spacious version of a low-maintenance home and pick a one-bedroom design. These can cover anywhere between 684 and 846 square feet. Our largest floor plans are the two-bedrooms, starting at 887 and going up to 1,252 square feet; they have all the space you need. To make life simple and stress-free, our homes come equipped with keyless Schlage entry systems, Nest Pro learning thermostats, solar water heating, and have a full-size washer and dryer included. More than that, based on the floor plan design you choose, your new apartment could have a private terrace, dry bar, dual vanity in the bathrooms, or other thoughtful additions.

Topping your Cannery Park by Windsor experience are the stunning community features. Spanning over 35,000 square feet, they include a pristine swimming pool, yoga lawn, grilling areas, and double-sided fireplace. There is also a clubhouse with a 24-hour fitness center, catering kitchen, cyber lounge with iMacs, and Smart TV lounge with billiards, among many others. We even added pet-wash stations for the comfort of your furry friends. Plus, we didn’t even mention how close we are to both Silicon Valley and downtown San Jose. Find out more and contact us for a tour!
